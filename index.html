How RiskRate Oracle Works

A seamless bridge from public relations to onchain capital allocation.

01

Data Aggregation

Our decentralized node operators continuously monitor public press releases and rating actions from major Nationally Recognized Statistical Rating Organizations (NRSROs). We index this public data in real-time.

02

Consensus & Attestation

The aggregated ratings are verified through a Byzantine Fault Tolerant consensus mechanism. Once verified, the data is pushed onchain as a cryptographically signed attestation attached to your company's verified wallet or smart contract identity.

03

DeFi Integration

Institutional DeFi lending protocols (e.g., Aave, Maker, Clearpool) query our oracle. Your verified high-grade rating automatically unlocks access to institutional borrowing pools, specific interest rate parameters, and liquidity.
